# What is BlankFX?

BlankFX is the stablecoin FX infrastructure layer for businesses that need fast settlement, tight pricing, and a clean audit trail.

## What it does

BlankFX is built for corridor-based settlement rather than generic token swapping. The focus is predictable execution, not broad DeFi trading.

Pricing is designed to stay tight, execution is routed for best price, and every transfer leaves a clear audit trail.

## Who it's for

- **Founders and operators** moving treasury balances across corridors
- **Web3 businesses** that need FX execution inside their product flow
- **Ops and treasury teams** that care about control, reconciliation, and traceability
